# Opposite Verbs
つける - to turn on
けす - to turn off

すわる - to sit down
たつ - to stand up

あける (開ける) - to open
しめる (閉める) - to close

〜に　のる - to ride, board (L5)
〜に　はいる - to enter
〜を　おりる - to get off

かりる - to borrow
かえす - to return (a thing)
- 〜に - person (receiving the thing?)
- 〜を - the thing being returned
